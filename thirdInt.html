Karlie Kloss

is a supermodel, entrepreneur, and the founder of Kode With Klossy, a girl's coding camp that teaches web and app development. Since we are Kode With Klossy scholars, we were able to get in contact with Karlie and ask her a quick question.

Why should people learn how to code?

I think everyone should learn how to code because it's important to understand the world around us and coding is the future!
